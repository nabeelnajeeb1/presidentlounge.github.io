Teams Meeting

Use the room's Microsoft Teams Room system for the best meeting experience.

1
Schedule a Microsoft Teams meeting.
2
Add the following room mailbox as a participant:
pihboardroom@powerholding-intl.com
3
The meeting will automatically appear on the room tablet.
4
Press Join on the room tablet.
5
The room system will automatically use:
  • MAXHUB Camera
  • Room Microphones
  • Room Speakers
  • Meeting Room Displays
6
Begin your meeting.
Recommended option for Microsoft Teams meetings.

BYOD / Zoom Call

Use your own laptop with Zoom, Webex, Google Meet, or any conferencing platform.

Method 1 — MAXHUB Wireless Dongle
1
Connect the MAXHUB wireless dongle to your laptop.
2
Launch MAXHUB Connect software.
3
If not installed, download and install it:
Windows Download Your OS
macOS Download Your OS

After installation:

1
Open MAXHUB Connect.
2
Follow the connection instructions.
3
Start your Zoom, Webex, Teams or Google Meet session.
Method 2 — USB-C Direct Connection
1
Connect the room USB-C cable to your laptop.
2
Accept the connection request shown on the room tablet.
3
The room camera, microphones and speakers will become available.
4
Start your preferred meeting platform.
Ideal for guests and visitors.
